## Contact us

The best way to reach us is by email:

**[finance.app.support01@gmail.com](mailto:finance.app.support01@gmail.com)**

We usually reply within **1–2 business days**. So we can help as quickly as possible, please include:

- Your device and OS version (for example, iPhone 15, iOS 18)
- Your app version
- A clear description of what happened, with screenshots if possible

You can also reach us straight from the app: open **Settings → Contact support**. This starts a pre-filled email that already includes your device model, OS version, and app version, so we have what we need to help.

## Frequently asked questions

### How do I add a transaction?

On the Home screen, tap the **+** button, choose **Income**, **Expense**, **Fixed expense**, or **Savings**, enter the amount, pick a category, and save. You can also set a transaction to repeat, so recurring bills are logged automatically.

### Is my financial data private?

Yes. Your financial data is stored **on your device**, not on our servers — we cannot see your expenses, income, budgets, savings, or receipts. The full details are in our [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY_POLICY).

### How do I back up or restore my data?

FinanceApp can save an **encrypted** backup to **your own** iCloud (iOS) or Google Drive (Android) account, and you can also export a backup file manually to store wherever you like. You'll find both options in **Settings**. Cloud backup is a Premium feature. Because backups are encrypted with a key held on your device, keep them safe — we cannot recover them for you.

### I bought Premium but don't see it (or I switched devices)

Open the **Membership** screen and tap **Restore Purchases**. Your purchase is tied to your Apple ID (or Google account), so restoring re-activates Premium on any device signed in to the same account.

### How do I delete my data?

You can erase everything from **Settings → Reset Plan**, or by uninstalling the app. Uninstalling removes all locally stored data.

### How does receipt scanning work?

Receipt scanning reads the amount, merchant, and category from a photo **entirely on your device** — receipt images are never uploaded. It needs camera or photo-library permission, which you can grant or decline at any time. Receipt scanning is a Premium feature.

### How do I manage notifications?

Open **Settings → Notifications** to turn budget alerts, savings reminders, and spending summaries on or off.
